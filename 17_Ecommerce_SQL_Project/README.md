# E-Commerce Sales Analytics Case Study

## Project Overview

This project is based on a real-world E-Commerce business scenario where SQL is used to analyze customer behavior, product performance, sales trends, and business growth.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful insights that help businesses make better decisions.

---

## Business Problem

An online retail company wants answers to the following questions:

- Which products generate the highest revenue?
- Which customers contribute the most sales?
- What are the monthly sales trends?
- Which product categories perform best?
- What is the average order value?
- Which products need attention due to low sales?

This project demonstrates how SQL can be used to solve these business challenges.

---

## Business Scenario

The company sells products through an online platform.

Customers place orders, purchase products, and make payments.

Management requires detailed reports and insights to improve sales performance and customer satisfaction.

---

## Database Design

### Customers
Stores customer information.

### Products
Stores product details and pricing.

### Categories
Stores product categories.

### Orders
Stores customer orders.

### Order_Items
Stores product-level order details.

### Payments
Stores payment transactions.
